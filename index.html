Vasthu

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science of architecture and construction that aims to create a harmonious living environment by balancing natural elements like earth, water, air, fire, and space. The primary objective is to ensure positive energy flow within the house, promoting health, prosperity, and happiness for the occupants.

Building Permit

A building permit is a formal approval from the local government or municipal authority that allows you to proceed with your construction project. It ensures that the building plans comply with local building codes, zoning regulations, and safety standards.

Panchayat Paper Work

Constructing a house within the jurisdiction of a Panchayath (local village council) in India requires certain approvals, documentation, and paperwork. The Panchayath is responsible for regulating land use, construction, and development activities within its limits.
